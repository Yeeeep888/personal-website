Photography

Photography is how I observe, pause, and frame the small truths in people, places, and petals. Each category tells a different part of the story.

📷 Portraits

Each face tells a story written in expressions, shadows, and light. My portrait work seeks to reveal the quiet truths behind the eyes of those who trust me with their image.

🏙️ Urban

Cities are living organisms with beating hearts and complex personalities. These captures explore the dialogue between architecture, humanity, and the passage of time in our constructed environments.

🌄 Landscapes

Nature's grandeur reminds us of our place in the universe. These images attempt to translate the overwhelming beauty of vast horizons and open skies into frames that evoke both wonder and peace.

🌿 Botanical

Plants teach us about resilience, growth, and finding beauty in cycles of renewal. My botanical photography celebrates the intricate designs and quiet wisdom found in leaves, petals, and stems.
